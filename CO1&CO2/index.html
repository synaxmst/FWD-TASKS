Most Asked Questions

What courses are offered by MST Career Institute?

MST Career Institute offers coaching for academic subjects, competitive examinations, career guidance programs, and skill development courses based on student requirements.

How can I contact the helpdesk for support?

Students can contact the helpdesk through email or phone during working hours. Queries can also be submitted using the online query form available on the website.

What should I do if I miss a class?

If a student misses a class, they can contact the helpdesk to get information about recorded sessions, backup classes, or study materials.

How are doubts cleared outside class hours?

Doubt clarification sessions are conducted regularly, and students can raise their doubts through the helpdesk or during scheduled doubt-clearing hours.

Is academic counseling available for students?

Yes, academic and career counseling is available to guide students in choosing the right courses and improving their performance.

What if I face technical issues during online classes?

Students facing technical issues can report them to the helpdesk, and support will be provided to resolve login or access-related problems.

How can I provide feedback about classes or faculty?

Students can submit their feedback through the feedback or query section of the website, which helps improve teaching quality and student support.
